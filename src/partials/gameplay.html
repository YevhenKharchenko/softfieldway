HOW TO PLAY

Liquid Out is easy to learn and instantly engaging.

  • Watch the colored liquid flowing through the pipes

  • Tap the matching bottle at the bottom

  • Open the valve at the perfect time

  • Catch the correct color before it changes

You really gotta watch closely and time it just right, or you'll mess up the whole thing.
At first, the flow is predictable. But as levels progress, pipes twist, merge, and overlap — requiring sharper focus and faster decisions.
Just tap with one finger, but there's tons you can do with it.
