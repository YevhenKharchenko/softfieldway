PROGRESSIVE CHALLENGE

From Easy Streams to Twisted Mazes

The early levels introduce basic mechanics and simple pipe paths.
Then:

  • Multiple colors flow simultaneously

  • Pipes overlap and cross

  • Speed increases

  • Flow becomes unpredictable

Sure, it gets tougher, but it's not the kind of hard that makes you want to chuck your phone across the room.
There's like a ton of levels - I'm still working through them and haven't gotten bored yet.
