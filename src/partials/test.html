Think Before You Tap

This isn’t random tapping — it’s strategy.
You’ll need to:

  • Predict which color comes next

  • Watch pipe direction changes

  • Plan ahead for mixed flows

  • Avoid tapping too early or too late

When the pipes start getting all twisted and crazy, you really have to think fast.
The more you play, the better you get at timing things just right and thinking on your feet.
